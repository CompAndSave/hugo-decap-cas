## **How to Download and Install the HP Printer App**

Setting up the HP Smart App is the first step to managing your printer efficiently. This app lets you print, scan, and monitor your printer from any device. By following a simple installation process, you can start connecting the HP printer to WiFi, manage ink levels, and even perform an [HP printer firmware update](https://www.compandsave.com/blog/posts/hp-printer-firmware-update-a-guide-for-smooth-safe-upgrades.html). 

### **Step 1: Visit 123.hp.com**

Go to [123.hp.com](https://123.hp.com) and enter your printer model. This ensures you download the correct app for your device.

### **Step 2: Check Available App**

If the HP App is available for your printer, install it. Otherwise, download HP Smart App. Both apps provide full printer functionality.

### **Step 3: Log in with Your HP Account**

Open the app and sign in with your HP account. This enables features like HP ink subscription activation and remote printing from your mobile or PC.

### **Step 4: Connect Your Printer**

Make sure your printer is powered on and connected to your network. The app will guide you through the HP App setup and walk you through on how to use HP Smart App features.

### **Troubleshooting Tips**

* Ensure your internet connection is stable.
* Restart your device if the app does not install.
* Check that your printer supports the app version.
