## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **1. Is a Brother monochrome laser printer better than an inkjet?**

Yes, especially if you mostly print text or documents. A Brother monochrome laser printer is faster, more reliable, and much cheaper per page than an inkjet. You won’t deal with dried ink or frequent cartridge changes, and the toner lasts longer. Inkjet printers are ideal for colorful photos, but for homework, forms, or business files, a Brother laser printer is the smarter long-term investment.

### **2. Can I use third-party toner in a Brother laser printer black and white?**

Yes. As long as it’s a compatible and trusted brand, third-party toner performs just as well as OEM toner, often at half the cost. Using high-quality compatible toner cartridges from CompAndSave gives you crisp, professional prints without the premium price tag. Plus, you’ll reduce waste since compatible toner cartridges can often be recycled or refilled multiple times.

### **3. How long does a toner cartridge last?**

A standard toner cartridge can print around 1,200 pages, while a high-yield version reaches up to 3,000 pages or more. That’s months, or even years of printing for most homes. Because toner doesn’t dry up like ink, your Brother b&w laser printer stays ready to print anytime, even after long breaks. It’s the ideal setup for families who print occasionally but still want sharp, dependable results.

### **4. What’s the difference between a monochrome laser printer and an all-in-one mono laser printer?**

A monochrome laser printer prints black-and-white only, while an all-in-one mono laser printer adds scanning and copying features. Both share the same efficient toner system and speed, but the all-in-one model saves space by combining multiple functions in a single machine, making it perfect for home offices or remote workers who need to manage paperwork easily.
