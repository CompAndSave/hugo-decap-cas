## **What Is an Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) and How Does It Work?**

An Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) is a printer feature that feeds multiple pages automatically for scanning, copying, or faxing. Instead of placing each page manually on the scanner glass, you load a stack of documents into the feeder.

It uses rollers and sensors to move each sheet. Failure can cause a paper jam, misfeeds, or wrinkling paper. Issues often appear as Brother printer feeder not working while the flatbed still works.

### **Simple Explanation of ADF Functionality**

The ADF grabs and feeds pages automatically into the printer. It detects paper size and thickness, and feeds each sheet separately. This prevents manual handling and speeds up large jobs.

### **How ADF Feeds Multiple Pages Automatically**

The rollers pick one sheet at a time from the stack. Sensors track the paper movement to avoid jams. Proper loading and fanning of documents are essential.

### **Why ADF Issues Disrupt Scanning and Copying**

When the Brother printer document feeder is not working, the printer may skip pages, fail to feed, or show errors. Dirty rollers, wrong paper type, or incorrect alignment often cause problems. Regular printer maintenance tips, like cleaning rollers and using supported paper, can prevent most issues.

## **Brother Printer Document Feeder Not Working: Common Reasons Explained**

A paper jam or feeding failure is a common reason for the Brother automatic document feeder not working. When the Brother printer's document feeder is not working, scanning and copying multiple pages becomes difficult. Here are the main reasons:

### **1. Unsupported Paper Size or Weight**

Using paper outside the supported dimensions or weight prevents the ADF from grabbing pages. Standard sizes like A4, Letter, and Legal with 17–24 lb (64–90 g/m²) work best. Thick, thin, or folded sheets can cause printer wrinkling paper.

### **2. Incorrect Document Loading (Orientation, Alignment)**

Documents must be face up, top edge first, and aligned with the guides. Misaligned pages cause skipped feeds or a Brother printer document feeder not working.

### **3. Dirty or Worn Paper Feed Rollers**

Dust, ink, or debris on rollers reduces grip. Cleaning the rollers is an essential printer maintenance tip. Worn rollers may need replacement.

### **4. Paper Jams or Debris Inside ADF**

Small scraps or stuck paper block the feeder. Removing debris prevents repeated paper jam errors and ensures smooth feeding.

### **5. Software/Settings Issues** 

Auto Crop in scanning software can bypass the ADF. Disabling it in ControlCenter or PaperPort fixes the Brother automatic document feeder not working during scans.

### **6. Faulty ADF Sensor or Hardware Issue**

If all else fails, the sensor or mechanism may be broken. Persistent issues indicate that the Brother printer feeder is not working and may be hardware-related, and may need professional repair.

## **Quick Checks Before You Fix Brother Automatic Document Feeder Issues**

Most Brother printer document feeder not working issues are caused by paper, loading, or user errors. Simple checks can prevent a paper jam and fix the Brother printer document feeder problem. These issues often appear as a Brother printer not loading paper.

### **1. Make Sure Paper Meets ADF Requirements**

Use supported sizes like Letter, A4, or Legal and weights between 17–24 lb (64–90 g/m²). Avoid wrinkled, folded, stapled, or damp paper. Correct paper prevents misfeeds and [printer wrinkling paper](https://www.compandsave.com/blog/posts/printer-wrinkling-paper-top-causes-fixes-and-prevention-tips.html).

### **2. Check Proper Document Loading in the ADF**

Load documents face up, top edge first. Adjust the paper guides to fit the stack. Misaligned pages can trigger the Brother printer document feeder not working error.

### **3. Avoid Common User Mistakes**

Do not overload the ADF (max 50 pages). Fan the paper stack before loading. Avoid unsuitable materials like envelopes, cardboard, or photo paper. Regular printer maintenance tips, like cleaning rollers, help maintain smooth operation.

## **Brother Automatic Document Feeder Not Working: Step-by-Step Troubleshooting**

When your Brother automatic document feeder is not working, it can cause a paper jam, skipped pages, or the printer failing to scan or copy. Most problems are simple to fix with step-by-step troubleshooting. These steps help identify whether the issue is related to setup, paper, rollers, sensors, or software. Following them also resolves the issues.

### **Step#1 Test ADF Using Copy Function**

Start by testing the ADF using the copy function. Load a few pages and check if the display shows “ADF Ready”. If it does, the hardware is working, and issues may be setup-related. If not, the problem could be mechanical or paper-related. This step helps pinpoint whether the Brother printer document feeder is not working is hardware or software-related.

### **Step#2 Inspect and Clear the ADF for Paper Jams**

Open the ADF cover. Remove any stuck paper, scraps, or debris. Make sure the paper path is smooth. Even small pieces can block the rollers and cause repeated paper jam errors or printer wrinkling of paper. This simple check often fixes the Brother automatic document feeder not working problem.

### **Step#3 Reset Your Brother Printer**

Turn off the printer and unplug it for 10 seconds. Plug it back in and power on. A simple reset can clear temporary errors and help fix the Brother printer document feeder not working issue caused by glitches.

### **Step#4 Clean Paper Feed Rollers and Pick-Up Rollers**

Dirty rollers reduce grip, causing the Brother printer feeder not to work. You’ll need a dry lint-free cloth, water, or rubbing alcohol. Remove the paper tray, clean all rollers with a moistened cloth, then wipe dry. Reinstall the tray and reload paper. Regular printer maintenance tips like this prevent misfeeds and Brother printer paper feed not working errors.

### **Step#5 Check ADF Sensor or Switch**

Locate the ADF sensor or switch inside the feeder. Press it to test. If the sensor is faulty, the Brother automatic document feeder not working issue persists even with correct paper and clean rollers. Signs include no ADF Ready message or skipped pages. Faulty sensors usually require professional repair.

### **Step#6 Fix Scanning Issues When ADF Is Not Working**

Sometimes the ADF works for copying but not scanning. This is often due to software settings, not hardware.

**Disable Auto Crop Setting (Common Fix)**

Auto Crop can bypass the ADF during scanning. Disable it in:

* ControlCenter4 (Home & Advanced Mode)
* Scan the key on the machine
* PaperPort software

After disabling Auto Crop, the ADF should scan correctly. This resolves many Brother printer document feeder complaints for scanning tasks.
