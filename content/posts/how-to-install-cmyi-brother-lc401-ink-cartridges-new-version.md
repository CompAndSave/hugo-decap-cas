## **How to Safely Remove Old LC401 Ink Cartridges**

Many printing issues occur because the old cartridge is removed incorrectly. If your Brother printer cannot detect cartridges, improper removal is often the cause. Knowing how to handle Brother ink cartridges safely ensures your Brother ink LC401 works smoothly and avoids damage to the printer.

### **Step-by-Step Removal Guide for CMYi Brother LC401 Ink Cartridge Version**

1. Open the ink cartridge cover of your printer.
2. For the **CMYi version**, there is no lever so pinch the cartridge instead to unlock it from the cartridge slot.
3. Gently pull out the cartridge without touching the nozzles or spilling ink.
4. Set the old cartridge aside for recycling or safe disposal.
5. Repeat for each color if replacing a full set of Brother LC401 LC401XL ink.

## **Troubleshooting Brother LC401 Recognition Issues**

Sometimes, your Brother printer cannot detect a cartridge even after proper installation. This issue sometimes happens with Brother LC401 cartridges and can interrupt printing. Understanding why this happens helps fix the problem quickly. Brother ink LC401 may fail recognition due to improper installation, protective tape left on, or using an incompatible cartridge.

### **Why Printers May Not Recognize Cartridges**

* **Incompatible cartridge**: Using a cartridge not designed for your printer model can trigger recognition errors.
* **Improper installation:** Cartridges not fully seated or misaligned may cause the printer to fail to detect ink.
* **Protective tape left on**: Leaving the tape on the ink nozzles blocks contact with the printer.
* **Mixed cartridge types:** Combining LC401 and LC401XL in the wrong slots may confuse the printer.

### **Quick Fixes for Recognition Issues**

1. **Reset the printer:** Turn it off, wait 30 seconds, then power on.
2. **Clean the contacts:** Use a lint-free cloth to gently wipe the cartridge and slot contacts.
3. **Reinstall cartridges:** Remove and reinstall the Compatible Brother LC401 ink cartridges.
4. **Check cartridge type:** Ensure you are using the correct LC401 or LC401XL for your printer.
