## **What Is Toner and Why Is Cheap Brother Toner Better Than Ink Cartridges**

A Brother toner cartridge uses powdered toner that is fused onto paper during printing. This process produces sharp, clean text with minimal waste. Because toner lasts longer, users searching for a cheap Brother laser printer toner benefit from fewer cartridge changes.

Brother printers use toner for speed, durability, and efficiency. Toner handles large print volumes better than ink. It also makes it easier to replace toner on a Brother printer without clogging or leaks. Choosing toner for Brother printers with a good yield means more prints and fewer interruptions.

### **Toner vs Ink Cartridge**

| Feature | Toner Cartridge  | Ink Cartridge |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Page Yield | High – prints thousands of pages | Low-prints a few hundred pages |
| Cost per Page | Low – more cost-effective for frequent printing | High–frequency replacements increase cost |
| Print Quality | Consistent, sharp text and images | Can fade quickly, inconsistent output |
| Durability | Does not dry out; long shelf life | Can dry out if unused; shorter lifespan |
| Replacement Frequency | Rare – fewer interruptions | Frequent – more maintenance needed |
| Best Use | Home, office, high-volume printing | Low-volume or occasional printing |
